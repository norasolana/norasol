About Us: Nora's Legacy

Our token, NORA, is inspired by the resilient and beloved polar bear from the Oregon Zoo. Born against the odds in 2015, Nora's story of overcoming health challenges and becoming a symbol of hope in wildlife conservation resonates with our mission.

NORA represents:

  • Resilience: Overcoming adversity.
  • Community: Building connections beyond borders.
  • Conservation: Advocating for our planet's future.

Join us, embracing Nora's spirit of endurance and community in the crypto world.
